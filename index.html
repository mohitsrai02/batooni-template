Trouble downloading or installing

Basic
1. Will I get money if I skip an ad?
Ans. No, Batooni only registers a view when the ad is completed. Therefore when you skip an ad, you don’t get paid.
2. Will I get money if the call does not connect?
Ans. Of course, you watch the whole ad and you get paid, that’s the deal.
3. Will I get money if I call on a toll- free number?
Ans. No, and neither would you see an ad when you dial a toll-free number.
4. My connection is post-paid, can I still use Batooni?
Ans. Of course, Batooni wallet can be used to pay your post-paid bills.
5. Can I recharge someone else’s number using my Batooni wallet?
Ans. Yes.
Steps to follow:-
- Switch off “Auto- Recharge” from the settings tab.
- Go to the wallet, make sure you have sufficient balance in you Batooni wallet.
- Click “Recharge” and change the details such as operator, phone number, and amount.
- Ta-da! It’s done.
6. Can I transfer my available wallet to another person?
Ans. No, but you can recharge their mobile phone account and that is as good.
7. Can I transfer my available balance to my Paytm account?
Ans. No, not yet but hang on, we are working on it.
8. How many ads can I watch every day?
Ans. Batooni promises a minimum of 7 ads per day, which means you can earn a minimum of INR 105/- per month. Please note that the maximum number of ads available would be subject to change.
9. What is screen overlay and why is it interfering with Batooni?
Ans. Phew! Screen overlay is a feature of your phone which does… well, that probably doesn’t matter. What matters is that it is common to face problems while installing other applications on your phone. Don’t worry, it doesn’t mean that our application is not safe. Please go to your settings tab and switch off- “Screen overlay” or disallow “drawing over other apps”
Here is a tutorial
10. I installed the application but cannot register, why?
Ans. There can be few scenarios when this can happen, let us figure it out together. - If the internet switched off?
That might be it, while the Batooni does not need the internet to show you ads, it does need connectivity to register and fetch data from the server. Please retry when you are in a Wi-Fi zone.
- Have you registered on Batooni earlier with the same number?
Batooni does not allow you to restore your account using the same number more than 3 times within 14 days.
11. I use Jio, how can Batooni help me?
Ans. If only we had a penny for each time someone asked us that question. If your bills are taken care of, spread happiness using Batooni. Know more on how (hyperlink to Batooni stories)
12. How much internet would Batooni need?
Ans. INR 17-20/- depending on the data rates applicable to you.

Wallet and recharge questions

Ad Views
1. I switched on the “Phone Dialler Feature” but no ads are shown.
Ans. Hmm, this is interesting. Let us try the age old method of restarting the app. If it does not work, we would request you to write to us at phonedialler@batooni.com with the details of your phone.
Meanwhile, please switch to Batooni Dialler and enjoy!
Recharge
2. I have seen 20 ads but did not receive any recharge, what can I do?
Ans. Don’t worry, your money is safe with us. Please check the following:
- It might be that the transaction is taking long. Open “Wallet “from the menu and check in the list of transactions, and it would show the status- “Pending”
- It might be that your auto-recharge is off. Open the wallet and check the available balance, it would be added to the wallet.
- It might be that you are not connected to the internet, please do so and recheck.
- If all else fails, write to us with screenshots at rechargefailed@batooni.com
3. I tried recharging from Batooni wallet but the recharge is failing.
Ans. There can be few scenarios when this can happen, let us figure it out together. Please check the following:
- Are you connected to the internet?
- Are you typing the correct phone number?
- Are you selecting the correct operator and network?
- Is recharge of the amount available for your network? For example, Airtel post-paid does not allow a payment of less than INR 50.
- Please retry in some time.
- If all else fails, please write to us with screenshots at rechargefailed@batooni.com
4. I have an internet connection but Batooni shows the message “Please connect to internet”, why?
Ans. Batooni needs to connect to its server once every 24 hours. Please try again in some time, we are sure it will sync and details such as ads and wallet would refresh.

Explain me the settings

Settings
1. I cannot understand my “ads details”.
Ans. Batooni believes in sharing with you all relevant details and this is part of us doing that. Ads remaining for today- Number of ads left for you to view today Total Ads available for today- Maximum number of ads that you can view today Ads remaining for next recharge- Number of ads you need to watch to get your next recharge
2. What is the auto-recharge feature?
Ans. If this is on, then after every 20 ads, your phone number will be recharged with INR 10. If you switch this off, this INR 10 will be added to your Batooni account. We will keep this away safely till you want.
3. I want to see ads while making a call from my phone’s dialler. How do I do that?
Ans. Simple.
- Go to your Setting tab by clicking on the top right of the Batooni Screen
- Switch on “Phone Dialler” feature
- Tada! It’s done.
